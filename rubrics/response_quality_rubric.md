# Response Quality Rubric

## Purpose

This rubric evaluates the overall quality of an AI-generated response.

## Total Score

25 points

| Category | Points |
|---|---:|
| Accuracy | 5 |
| Completeness | 5 |
| Clarity | 5 |
| Reasoning Quality | 5 |
| Helpfulness | 5 |

## Accuracy

**5:** Fully accurate with no factual errors  
**4:** Mostly accurate with minor omissions  
**3:** Generally correct but incomplete  
**2:** Contains notable inaccuracies  
**1:** Mostly incorrect  
**0:** Misleading or harmful

## Completeness

**5:** Fully answers the prompt  
**4:** Covers most key points  
**3:** Covers some important points  
**2:** Misses major requirements  
**1:** Barely addresses the task  
**0:** Does not answer the task

## Clarity

**5:** Clear, organized, and easy to follow  
**4:** Mostly clear  
**3:** Understandable but could be better structured  
**2:** Confusing in places  
**1:** Difficult to understand  
**0:** Unclear

## Reasoning Quality

**5:** Strong reasoning with logical support  
**4:** Good reasoning with minor gaps  
**3:** Basic reasoning present  
**2:** Weak reasoning  
**1:** Unsupported conclusion  
**0:** No reasoning

## Helpfulness

**5:** Highly useful and actionable  
**4:** Useful with minor gaps  
**3:** Somewhat useful  
**2:** Limited usefulness  
**1:** Barely useful  
**0:** Not useful

## Final Rating

| Score | Rating |
|---:|---|
| 22-25 | Excellent |
| 18-21 | Good |
| 13-17 | Fair |
| 7-12 | Poor |
| 0-6 | Unacceptable |
