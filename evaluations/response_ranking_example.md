# Response Ranking Example

## Prompt

A user asks: "What should I do if my laptop is very slow during Zoom meetings?"

## Response A

You should restart your laptop, close unnecessary applications, check your internet connection, update Zoom, and make sure your operating system is up to date. If the issue continues, contact IT support with details such as your device model, connection type, and when the issue occurs.

## Response B

Your laptop is slow because it probably has a virus. Delete all large files and reinstall everything.

## Evaluation

| Category | Response A | Response B |
|---|---:|---:|
| Accuracy | 5 | 1 |
| Completeness | 4 | 1 |
| Clarity | 5 | 3 |
| Reasoning | 4 | 1 |
| Helpfulness | 5 | 1 |
| Total | 23 | 7 |

## Winner

Response A

## Justification

Response A provides safe, practical, and structured troubleshooting steps. It avoids unsupported assumptions and recommends escalation if basic troubleshooting fails. Response B makes an unsupported diagnosis and gives potentially disruptive advice without proper evidence.

## Suggested Improvement for Response A

Add steps for checking CPU usage, testing internet speed, and switching to a wired connection if available.
